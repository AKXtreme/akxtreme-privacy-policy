AKXtreme

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

AKXtreme is a local privacy and security application. All data — including your PIN, patterns, encrypted files, and app lock settings — is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service.

Data We Collect

AKXtreme does not collect any personal data. The following is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your PIN or pattern (stored as a one-way cryptographic hash — cannot be reversed)
  • List of apps you choose to lock
  • Files you import into the vault (encrypted with AES-256-GCM using your PIN)
  • Intruder photos captured when enabled (stored only on your device)
  • App preferences and settings

None of this data ever leaves your device.

Permissions & Why We Use Them

  • Accessibility Service — Detects when a locked app opens so the lock screen can be shown. Does NOT read screen content, keystrokes, or any data inside other apps.
  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) — Identifies the foreground app as a secondary detection method. No usage data is stored or transmitted.
  • Draw Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — Displays the lock screen overlay on top of locked apps.
  • Camera — Captures a photo when the wrong password is entered (Intruder Detection). Only used when explicitly enabled by you. Photos are stored locally.
  • Device Admin — Prevents the app from being uninstalled without your password. Optional feature.
  • Storage (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO) — Allows you to select files to import into the encrypted vault.
  • Notifications — Shows a silent background notification required by Android to keep the protection service running.
  • Vibration — Haptic feedback during PIN or pattern input.

Encryption

Files stored in the vault are encrypted using AES-256-GCM with keys derived from your PIN via PBKDF2WithHmacSHA256 (100,000 iterations). Your PIN is never stored — only a cryptographic hash is saved for verification. Encrypted files cannot be recovered without the correct PIN.

Data Sharing

AKXtreme does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting tools included in this app. No internet permission is requested or used.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored on your device and under your control. You can:

  • Delete individual vault files at any time from within the app
  • Clear all app data via Android Settings → Apps → AKXtreme → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device

Children's Privacy

AKXtreme does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not contain advertising, social features, or external communications of any kind.
