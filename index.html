AKxtreme Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

AKxtreme – App Lock & Vault is a mobile application designed to protect user privacy by providing app locking and secure private vault features.

Information Collection and Use

AKxtreme does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. All data processed by the app remains on the user's device.

Permissions Used

  • Accessibility Service: Used to detect when selected apps are opened in order to apply app lock protection. This service is used strictly for locking functionality and does not collect or share any personal data.
  • Storage Access: Used to securely store and manage private photos and videos within the vault. Files are stored locally on the device.
  • Overlay Permission (Display over other apps): Required to display the lock screen over protected applications.
  • Vibration Permission: Used to provide haptic feedback during PIN or pattern input.

Data Security

All files stored in the vault are encrypted and stored locally on the device. The app does not transmit any data to external servers.

User Control

Users have full control over their files. Files can be imported into the vault and can also be restored back to device storage at any time.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
